# NagrikAI — Project Overview\n\n## What is NagrikAI?

NagrikAI is Bangladesh's first **voice-activated civic AI agent** — a production-grade mobile application that lets ordinary citizens report civic issues, access emergency services, understand local laws, and stay safe — all through natural Bangla voice commands.

**Core thesis:** *"Zero-UI voice input with transparent agentic reasoning & autonomous action."*

---

## Key Differentiators

| Feature | Traditional Apps | NagrikAI |
|---|---|---|
| Input | Manual form typing | Voice in Bangla/dialects |
| Processing | Static | Live AI reasoning shown on screen |
| Emergency | Manual dialing | Auto-detected + routed instantly |
| Forms | Fill yourself | AI auto-fills from voice |
| Law help | Search engines | AI legal assistant (BD law) |
| Location | Passive | Active risk zone alerts |

---

## Target Users

- Citizens of Bangladesh (Dhaka initially, all BD later)
- People with low digital literacy
- Emergency situations (injury, flood, crime)
- Civic complaint reporters
- People needing legal guidance
